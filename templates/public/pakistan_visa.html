Preparation checklist

Common Items Travelers May Need

The exact list depends on visa category and nationality. The official portal’s requirements take priority.

  • Passport with sufficient remaining validity
  • Recent photograph meeting portal specifications
  • Travel itinerary and planned dates
  • Hotel booking or address in Pakistan where requested
  • Supporting or invitation letter when applicable
  • Debit or credit card for the official application fee
  • Additional documents requested for your visa category
